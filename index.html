Ballerina Cappuccina is a fun and bold meme-token built for the community. Inspired by Italian absurdity, espresso, and pure movement energy. BALERINA is not just a token – it's a lifestyle!

This project was created by my son, inspired by Italian meme culture. It fills him with joy to see how warmly people have reacted to the idea. We are deeply grateful to everyone who has shown support for Ballerina Cappuccina.

24 hours of purchase — this is a temporary measure to stabilize the price in the early phase, as the initial liquidity pool is small and very sensitive to price movements.

Additionally, a small 0.1% tax is applied to all transactions. These fees will be used to support the project and will flow back into the liquidity pool.

The contract has several protective measures:

  • No new tokens can be minted.
  • Liquidity pool cannot be withdrawn.
  • Ownership is retained only during the early phase to ensure no malicious actions can harm the community.

Once the project reaches a stable and mature state, all special privileges and controls will be renounced. This is a community token — your trust and enthusiasm are everything.
